About Me

Hi! I'm Stella, a high school student, programmer, and aspiring engineer with a passion for robotics, software development, and solving challenging problems.

As a member of my FIRST Robotics Competition team, I contribute to the development of robot software, autonomous routines, and data analysis tools that help improve performance both on and off the field. I also serve as our team's Outreach Lead, where I organize community engagement efforts, build partnerships, and help inspire the next generation of students to explore STEM through robotics.

I enjoy working with Java and Python, building projects that combine programming, engineering, and data to turn ideas into reality. Whether I'm developing software, analyzing match statistics, or collaborating with teammates on new ideas, I love tackling challenges and learning something new along the way.

Outside of robotics, I enjoy reading, writing, playing tennis, and exploring new technologies. From experimenting with Raspberry Pis to creating personal programming projects, I'm always looking for opportunities to grow, create, and make a positive impact through technology.

As I continue my journey in engineering and computer science, I'm excited to keep learning, collaborating with others, and using technology to solve real-world problems.
